How licensing should feel

授权体验应该是什么样

  • activation should be local and understandable激活过程应当本地化且易懂
  • trial or grace periods should be explicit试用期或宽限期要明确说明
  • license failures should not make the whole UI unusable授权失败不应导致整个界面不可用
  • users should know how many devices are included用户应知道可激活多少台设备
