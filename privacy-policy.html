Privacy Policy for TraceFence

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Thank you for using TraceFence ("we", "our", or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how TraceFence handles information when you use the desktop app and its direct-download releases.

Key Principle: TraceFence is designed to keep core monitoring and product data on your device. We do not need a hosted account or an analytics stack for the default experience.

1. Information We Process

TraceFence is designed as a local-first application. The app may process the following data on your device:

2. Optional External Services

If you choose to connect an external provider, TraceFence will explain what data is sent, where it goes, and why it is needed. Optional services must remain opt-in.

We do not operate a mandatory cloud service for core product usage.

3. Data Storage

App data is stored locally on your device in the application support area used by the desktop build. The exact path may vary by platform and packaging format.

Stored data may include cache files, preferences, licensing metadata, and locally generated summaries. You can remove them by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

4. Network Access

TraceFence only uses network access when a feature explicitly requires it, such as downloading a release update, verifying a license, or connecting to an external provider you chose.

5. Third-Party Services

Any third-party provider you connect is responsible for its own privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their policies before using the integration.

6. Your Choices

  • view your data inside the app
  • clear local caches and summaries
  • disconnect optional services
  • remove the app to delete local data

7. Children's Privacy

TraceFence is not directed to children under 13 and is not designed to knowingly collect information from them.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when the product or release model changes. The latest version will be published in this repository and can also be linked from the website.
